Declaration of Principles

1. Worldwide Shelters provides shelters that can function in the immediate wake of a disaster,
    while later becoming the frame of a permanent home

2. Worldwide Shelters is focused on helping people rebuild their lives in the wake of war and
    natural disasters.

3. Worldwide Shelters believes shelter is at the forefront of human need, right alongside
    the need for food, water, and medicine.

4. Worldwide Shelters is dedicated to innovative shelter design, ethical sourcing, and
    implementing responsive processes that speed relief to the people in need.

5. Worldwide Shelters believes collaboration is key and happily acts as the glue for partnering
    relief organizations, helping large groups operate efficiently and implement effectively.

6. Worldwide Shelters believes an emergency shelter that can transition into a semi-permanent
    dwelling is a more sustainable alternative to the traditional relief tent. It also gives beneficiaries
    a sense of ownership and the opportunity to improve the product in ways we might
    never imagine.

7. Worldwide Shelters will teach beneficiaries how to make shelter themselves, developing
    cottage industries and leaving behind something greater than a home.

8. Everything Worldwide Shelters does is thoughtfully developed – our goals, our products,
    our processes.
