About Us

There has never been an organization like Worldwide Shelters — a not-for-profit that works in partnership with other leading aid organizations to deliver a holistic approach to rebuilding communities ravaged by war and disaster. Shelter is the third largest spend in humanitarian aid but the most underfunded simply because, compared to food and medicine relief, there are far fewer organizational resources available for the shelter effort.

Worldwide Shelters strives to provide high value, thoughtfully developed shelters that not only put a roof over people's heads in the short-term but also help them transition toward a return to permanent housing. Our unique attributes include the following:

  • Provision of shelters that can function in the immediate wake of a disaster while later
    becoming the frame of a permanent home
  • Efficient delivery of relief services in the wake of disasters through collaboration with    
    many international aid agencies and governments
  • Innovative design of high-quality aid/relief shelters
  • Procurement and supply chain management services at cost to all qualified
    relief agencies
  • Unparalleled quality control oversight
