Partnerships

Working in partnership with other leading aid organizations, Worldwide Shelters has developed a breakthrough, holistic approach to rebuilding communities ravaged by war and disaster. Within those partnerships, one specific role for Worldwide Shelters is to provide and implement high-quality transitional shelter as part of a secure, healthy environment that provides privacy and dignity for those who live there. To accomplish this, Worldwide Shelters has created uniquely adaptable shelters.

Not surprisingly, the Worldwide Shelters approach is finding a receptive and collaborative audience with many international aid agencies and governments. According to shelter experts Chuck Setchell of USAID/OFDA and Rod Imer, World Vision's Global Shelter Manager, "the first step in whatever we do in the emergency phase hasgot to be the first step to permanent housing."

International organizations including the UN High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR), International Organization for Migration (IOM/OIM) and the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC) as well as private aid agencies such as World Vision, Save the Children, Doctors Without Borders/Mèdecins Sans Frontières (MSF), the International Rescue Committee (IRC), MedAir and Habitat for Humanity recognize the value of the Worldwide Shelters approach and have deployed Worldwide Shelters shelters in projects they have funded or conducted.

In some places, Worldwide Shelters has functioned largely as the provider of shelters or components to the international aid group that is the primary implementing organization at that site. In other places, we have worked in close partnership with the primary implementing agency. In the period for which we are now planning and fundraising, Worldwide Shelters will begin taking a leading role at selected sites and serving as the implementing agency. This initiative includes leading design, manufacturing and distribution of shelters along with site selection, land preparation and integration to help rebuild villages and communities ravaged by disasters. This initiative will signify a breakthrough approach to the humanitarian shelter sector and redefine the way humankind thinks about sheltering displaced populations.
