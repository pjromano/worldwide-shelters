Sector Leadership

Teamwork
As pioneers of better, more efficient and more effective shelter solutions, we have always advocated collaboration. Worldwide Shelters works with our partners to identify opportunities for maximizing our shelters’ benefits to displaced persons. This includes identifying potential design changes, as well as opportunities for value engineering so our cost reductions can be passed on to benefit the humanitarian community. We truly value the relationships we have with our partners. And we commit to being there for our partners at a moment’s notice, whenever and wherever we are needed: at headquarters, regional offices, distribution facilities, or relief sites.

Logistical Support
Worldwide Shelters warehouses relief shelters to strategically meet critical response needs. Stockpiles are tactically placed in Asia, the Middle East, Africa, Central America and the U.S. We also have relationships with multiple transportation companies to ship via surface or air to the designated location.

Financial Integrity
As a not-for-profit, Worldwide Shelters provides open accounting to its customers. We rely on grants and donations to support basic programs and administrative costs. Other product costs are recovered from the humanitarian organizations that use the shelters. We provide them with all invoices supporting product, shipping, and handling costs.

Design Expertise
Worldwide Shelters has internal and external design and engineering specialists who consult, conceptualize and develop high-quality shelter designs.

Prototyping Expertise
Worldwide Shelters offers customers prototyping expertise from concept and CAD design to initial product review with the ability to meet concise timetables.

Production Excellence
Worldwide Shelters has strong relationships with its contract manufacturing facilities built on deep collaboration and trust. These factories are ISO-qualified and our partners firmly stand behind their quality. Worldwide Shelters also has proven capability to establish production in additional locations, including developing countries in Africa and Asia.
