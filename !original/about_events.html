Raise Shelter, Raise Hope
Inaugural Event
Hosted by Michael and Lesley Salandra

 

Details

  • Due to ongoing commitments regarding
    our Haiti operation, Worldwide Shelters
    has chosen to postpone the Raise Shelter,
    Raise Hope Event at this time.

Please join us and contribute to the beginning of something truly inspiring. With 42 million people around the world who are without a home, the global shelter crisis is real. At this event, we'd like to share details of our revolutionary solutions to this problem, which are truly unique. By contributing to our cause, you will become a part of the international community dedicated to providing high value, thoughtfully developed shelter to victims of natural and manmade disasters worldwide.

Our goal is to increase an advocacy base of people who are passionate about the cause and assisting us in fighting this daunting crisis. We have a wonderful event planned, filled with live music and great food. We hope you and your friends will join us.
