Series 1100 Transitional Shelter

Dimensions:
Overall - 4.9m x 4.9m (16'W x 16'L)
Center Height - 2.2m (7'10")
Eave Height - 1.5m (4'8")
Floorspace - 24m sq (256' sq)

Fabric:
Highly durable 12.5 oz/yd2, 50/50 Polyester/Cotton, UV and Mildew Resistant, FR to CPAI-84

Colors:
White, tan, or blue (or as requested)

Weight:
192.8 kg (425 lbs)

Description
The Series 1100 is a representation of our mission to provide a more durable and superior shelter to ease the number of people separated from their basic human needs. This product is one of the most impressive structures available today and will outperform any other product on the market.

A key feature to our shelters' structural integrity is that the frame without the tent can be used as a substructure for a more permanent dwelling.

Features
Doors
  • Two doors with #10 zippers; each door has two windows (one per panel) measuring
    49.5cm x 97.8cm (19.5"W x 38.5"H)

Windows
  • Windows have fiberglass screens and full-cover flaps, which can be closed on three sides
    with Velcro and rolled up and secured with tie tapes; two windows, 222.2cm x 33cm
    (87.5"W x 13.5"H), are placed at opposite ends of tent walls for maximum cross ventilation;
    window flaps close on three sides with 2.5cm (1") Velcro and may be rolled up and tied in
    place with tie tapes

  • Fiberglass screen

  • Stovepipe shield has 12.7cm (5") round opening; entire shield covers an area of 40.6cm x
    43.2cm (16"W x 17"H) and has a flap that ties back when a stove is used or closes with
    Velcro if there is no stove

  • Tent has full-length reinforcement across the ridge

  • Six grommets on each eave for tie-down ropes

  • Four grommets on each side at bottom of tent walls for stakeout points; five grommets front
    and rear for this purpose

  • 5.1cm (2") Velcro on all sides of tent, 19cm (7.5") from the bottom of the walls — used to
    attach bathtub floor to wall

  • Interior partition allows privacy among occupants

  • Includes 12 ropes, approximately 3m (10') long with 22.9cm (9") steel stakes to be used for
    optional reinforcement

  • Weather-resistant frame constructed of 50mm (2") square steel tubing, 15 gauge, G90
    galvanized; frame can be anchored to the ground through 16mm (5/8") holes in the frame

  • Frame without tent can be used as a substructure for a more permanent dwelling

  • Designed to sleep six comfortably
