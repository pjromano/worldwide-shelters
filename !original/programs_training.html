In-Country Training

In order to be maximally effective as a provider of transitional shelters, Worldwide Shelters will need to train partner agency staff, including locally engaged personnel, in the use of the shelters as both short-term housing and as a valuable resource to be used in moving families back toward more permanent housing. To do this, it will be necessary to make in-country training (being piloted in Haiti) a routine part of all future Worldwide Shelters projects.
