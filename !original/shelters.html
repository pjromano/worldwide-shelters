Relief Shelters

As the shelter solutions-based organization, Worldwide Shelters deals with post-crisis sheltering — from delivering emergency tents in the initial 24-hour response through providing long-term transitional shelters that become the basis for building permanent structures.

Our goal has always been to provide the most functionally advanced, sustainable and ethically sourced temporary and transitional shelters available. That focus drives our products and positions us at the very forefront of shelter development.

As design innovators,Worldwide Shelters is always ready, willing and able to build custom shelters suitable for any specified environment, in any quantity, for any part of the world.

We are constantly developing new ideas and working with other shelter providers to meet new standards.

Current Worldwide Shelters shelter solutions include the following:

   Series 1100 Transitional Shelter
   Series 2100 Emergency Shelter + Winterization Kit
   Series 4100 Lightweight Emergency Shelter Tent
