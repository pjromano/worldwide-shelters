Raise Shelter, Raise Hope
 

At the end of last year, before the Haiti and Chile earthquakes, the total number of people forcibly uprooted by war, civil conflict and persecution around the world stood at 42 million. That included 16 million refugees and asylum seekers and 26 million people displaced within their own countries. Add to that the millions of Haitians and hundreds of thousands of Chileans displaced by earthquake. This growing number of international disasters and crises has made it even more imperative for the international community to come together to provide high value, thoughtfully developed shelter to victims of natural and manmade disasters. Join Worldwide Shelters in our mission to provide those without a roof over their head, better shelter faster.
